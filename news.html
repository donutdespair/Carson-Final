We’re Hiring!

09.14.16

So far in 2016 Unbearable Games released six indie games in the span of eight months. We also kept growing our thriving virtual fan community, moved into a new studio space in DUMBO, and began exploring mobile development.

As we plan ahead for 2016 and beyond, we are looking to grow our team. We’re planning to build out our existing core teams and expand to new areas.

Have a look at our hiring page to see which opportunity is right for you. If you decide to apply, you’ll be joining a unique organization, with a collaborative approach to building beautiful and intriguing products.

Take to the streets on on October 1st!

08.29.16

It’s almost time! In a little over a month Velo-City is coming to a computer near you. In this exciting new game, you will play as a bike messenger battling your way through New New York in 2099. It's a lot like being a bike messenger today, except you’ll be battling mutants and exploring the future! This game will be dangerous, grimey, low-paying, and with even more opportunities for bike justice!

Velo-City is the ultimate RPG experience. Not only is the landscape breathtakingly decrepit, but we’ve created a dramatic profile for New New York: glaring lights, creepy allies, and even a cameo by pizza rat. Vehicles break down, parts fall off, and customers explode in exciting new ways!
